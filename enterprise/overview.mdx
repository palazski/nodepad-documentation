NodePad Enterprise is for teams that want the NodePad workflow inside their own environment. You deploy the application stack, bring the backing services it needs, and control how provider access is configured.

<Note>
  Enterprise is separate from the hosted beta. The beta runs on NodePad-managed infrastructure. Enterprise runs in yours.
</Note>

## What to plan for

An enterprise deployment is not only "run these containers." You should plan for:

- PostgreSQL
- Redis
- S3-compatible object storage
- provider credentials and egress policy
- your upgrade and rollback process
- your identity, access, and compliance requirements

## Who this is for

Enterprise is a fit when you want the NodePad product shape but need deployment control. That usually includes:

- internal infrastructure ownership
- controlled provider access
- offline or restricted-network installation paths
- explicit change management around upgrades
