NodePad Enterprise is for teams that want the NodePad workflow inside their own environment. You deploy the application stack, bring the backing services it needs, and control how provider access is configured.

<Info>
  Enterprise is separate from the hosted beta. The beta runs on NodePad-managed infrastructure. Enterprise runs in yours.
</Info>

## What ships today

  <Card title="Self-hosting" icon="server">

## Who this is for

Enterprise is a fit when you want the NodePad product shape but need deployment control. That usually includes internal infrastructure ownership, controlled provider access, offline or restricted-network installation paths, and explicit change management around upgrades.
