NodePad Enterprise is a self-hosted deployment of NodePad for organizations that cannot run their AI workspace on a shared SaaS. You install NodePad in your own environment — Kubernetes or a single VM — and connect it to your own database, object store, and provider accounts. Conversation data never touches NodePad-managed infrastructure.

<Note>
  NodePad Enterprise is separate from the public beta. The beta runs on NodePad-managed cloud infrastructure. Enterprise deployments run on **your** infrastructure, under **your** control.
</Note>

## What Enterprise does NOT include yet

NodePad is in early beta. Several features you might expect on an enterprise tier are **not** shipped in v0.1:

- **SSO / SCIM / directory sync.** Authentication is email + password via Django's session-based auth. SSO (SAML 2.0 / OIDC) and SCIM provisioning are on the roadmap but not available today.
- **Role-based access control.** Workspaces are single-owner. There are no multi-user roles, permissions, or sharing primitives in the current release.
- **Audit logs.** Standard application logs exist; a dedicated audit trail of user actions is roadmap.
- **Local / self-hosted model inference.** NodePad today calls Anthropic or OpenAI directly. Routing through self-hosted Ollama, vLLM, Bedrock, or Vertex endpoints is roadmap.

We document what ships, not what's planned. If one of the above is a blocker for your deployment, tell us during the Enterprise conversation — timelines depend on pilot demand.

## Who Enterprise is for today

NodePad Enterprise v0.1 is the right fit for:

- Small and mid-sized teams who want AI on a canvas inside their own VPC or on-prem environment
- Organizations whose compliance posture requires that conversation data stays on their own infrastructure
- Teams planning a controlled rollout of canvas-based AI workflows before broader enterprise-identity integration arrives

It is **not** the right fit today for organizations that require SSO-only authentication, multi-user RBAC at the workspace level, or fully air-gapped model inference. Those are on the roadmap — reach out if you want to be part of shaping them.
