## How NodePad works

NodePad is built around three simple moves:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Chat as usual">
    Start with a single message and a single reply — just like any AI app. No new concepts required to get going.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Extend to multiple chats">
    When a question deserves its own thread, spawn a new chat right on the canvas. Related threads stay neighbors, not lost browser tabs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Control the context">
    Fork any message to branch the conversation, drop sticky notes to lock in constraints, and merge threads once you've reached a conclusion.
  </Step>
</Steps>
