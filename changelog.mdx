## v0.1.0 — pre-release

NodePad has not yet shipped its first tagged release. The first tag will include the canvas, branching, references, merge flows, text notes, model selection, and the self-hosting Helm chart.

Until `v0.1.0` ships, the canonical engineering log lives on [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/palazski/nodepad-api/releases). After the first tag, releases will be summarised on this page.
