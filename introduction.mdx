NodePad is an AI workspace built around a canvas instead of a single transcript. You still send prompts and get replies, but you can also branch from any message, keep separate lines of thought visible at once, and carry context across the canvas when you need it.

<SectionHeading eyebrow="Why NodePad" title="The core idea" />

Linear chat is fine until the work stops being linear. Research splits into sub-questions. Debugging needs a second opinion. Writing needs a fact-check side path that should not leak back into the draft. In a normal chat app, all of that gets tangled together.

NodePad gives that work a shape:
