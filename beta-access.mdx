NodePad is currently in beta. Access is free, but it is still managed in waves so the team can onboard new users deliberately and keep the product stable while features are still moving quickly.

## How to join

Visit [node-pad.com/beta](https://node-pad.com/beta) and submit your email address.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When will NodePad open to everyone?">
    There is no public general-availability date yet. Joining the beta list is the best way to get access before a broader launch.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is the beta free?">
    Yes. The current beta is free.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What should I assume about my data during beta?">
    Treat the hosted beta as an evolving product. Your workspaces and settings persist across sessions, but you should avoid storing anything irreplaceable without your own export or backup plan.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does it take to get invited?">
    It depends on demand and the current invite wave. Some users get in quickly. Others may wait longer.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can my team evaluate NodePad together?">
    Yes. For coordinated access or self-hosted discussions, reach out through the beta or enterprise contact path and describe what your team needs.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there an enterprise path?">
    Yes. NodePad has a self-hosted enterprise deployment path. Start with the [Enterprise overview](/enterprise/overview) if you want deployment details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
